A leader with a proven history
of service. Here are my goals

Economic Progress

Attract well-compensated jobs in our city and maintain fiscal accountability of taxpayer funds

Education

Continue our partnership with the school district to provide the best possible Education to our diverse community. Also provide grants for social and emotional initiatives that benefit our school children and families

Accomodation

Continue to enact policies that provide more affordable housing and collaborate with the county to assist our unhoused residents

Emergency Services

Continue to have a comprehensive well trained and well funded fire and emergency services department to protect and serve our community

Recreation

Enhance and maintain our parks and streetscapes. Provide opportunities for youth sports. Plan for a future Community Museum and Theatre for the Performing arts

Law Enforcement

Continue to have a comprehensive well trained and funded Police Department to respond to crimes in a timely manner in order to protect and serve our community
